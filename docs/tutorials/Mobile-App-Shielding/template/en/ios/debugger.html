Security Warning

Dear valued customer, we terminated mobile banking app due to a security issue.

What happened?

We identified that a debugger was connected to a running application.

What is the risk?

Debugger can read information from the mobile app, including sensitive data, such as an account list, transaction history, or even your PIN code. Debugger can also insert data in the application or launch a foreign code. As a result, payment or consent, for example, can be approved without your knowledge.

What could have caused the issue?

Debugger can connect to the application because of one of these reasons:

  • Your device is jailbroken and some of the non-standard apps contains built-in debugger in order to read data of other apps.
  • You are running an app on unofficial or beta version of mobile operating system that uses debugger to collect additional data of running apps.
  • You actively try to debug our application.

How to fix the issue?

To resolve the issue, make sure that:

  • You use official version of iOS.
  • Your device is not jailbroken (removal of OS protection).
  • You installed mobile app from App Store.
  • You do not run app in simulator or other non-standard runtime environment.
  • You do not actively debug our app using software development tools.
